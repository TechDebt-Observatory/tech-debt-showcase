# Ethical Considerations

## Our Commitments

### 1. Transparency
- All methods publicly documented
- No hidden processes or selective reporting
- Limitations clearly stated

### 2. Accuracy
- Manual validation of all findings
- False positives disclosed
- Corrections published when errors found

### 3. Respect for Maintainers
- No shaming or attacking developers
- Recognize constraints maintainers work under
- Frame as helpful, not critical
- Technical debt is normal, not shameful

### 4. Privacy
- Project operates anonymously
- No tracking beyond aggregate analytics
- Contact information protected
- User data not collected or sold

### 5. Non-Commercial
- No products being sold
- No advertising revenue
- No data harvesting
- Pure research/educational purpose

### 6. Open Source Values
- Methodology is open source
- Others can reproduce and validate
- Community contributions welcome
- Results benefit everyone

## What We Don't Do

❌ Cherry-pick results to look good  
❌ Hide false positives  
❌ Attack or shame maintainers  
❌ Sell data or consulting  
❌ Make claims beyond our evidence  
❌ Pretend AI is perfect  

## Conflicts of Interest

**None**. This project:
- Has no commercial sponsors
- Receives no funding
- Has no affiliation with:
  - OpenSSL project
  - Anthropic (Claude's creator)
  - Any competing projects
  - Security vendors
  - Consulting firms
