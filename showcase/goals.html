A Modest Proposal for our Educators

üéñÔ∏è The Military Model That Works

Before I touched a SH-60B Seahawk helicopter, I spent approximately 16 weeks in schools.

We learned to:

  • Read every schematic and system diagram
  • Understand how every component worked
  • Fix problems in simulators (not on real aircraft)
  • Document everything we did
  • Explain our reasoning to instructors

Only then were we allowed to work on actual helicopters.

Result: Near-zero failure rate. Lives depended on it.
